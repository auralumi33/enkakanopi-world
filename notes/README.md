# notes — the intake

A piece begins here, as a raw note in Rika's own hand.

- `inbox/` — drop a note. **Untracked** (gitignored): raw notes never ship.
- `redlines/` — where the tending pass leaves a structured, redlined draft for
  Rika to accept or reject. Also untracked.

Only after Rika approves does a piece leave this folder and grow into
[`../src/content/`](../src/content/) at its honest stage. Nothing skips the note,
and nothing skips her yes. See [`../ARCHITECTURE.md`](../ARCHITECTURE.md).
