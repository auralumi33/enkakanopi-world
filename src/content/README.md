## What if I want to quote something?

Yes. A sentence or two, with a link, is a normal citation. If you're
writing about the site — a review, a mention, a critique — quote what
you need, attribute to Fang Rika, link. If you want to reproduce a full
essay somewhere else, please ask first: `hello@enkakanopi.world`.

## What about AI training?

The site's public [`robots.txt`](/robots.txt) currently allows most
training crawlers (the design goal is citation reach — named frameworks
with one canonical source). But CC BY-NC-ND 4.0 governs the *reuse* of
what a model has seen: attribution required, non-commercial,
no derivatives. If your model surfaces a passage in a response, cite
"Fang Rika, enka kanopi" with a link back.
